Our Services

In/Outdoor Lighting

Whether you're in the midst of home renovations or constructing a new residence, you'll require an effective lighting system. We are proficient in providing great estimates for all your lighting repairs and installations.

Light Switches & Outlets

Aged light switches and outlets may necessitate replacement if they display malfunctions, generate heat upon touch, or deliver small shocks upon contact. Electric Al excels in replacing outdated switches and outlets and installing new ones in various locations, including outdoor and garage settings.

Home Automation

Experience the convenience of a smart home with our home automation solutions. Control your lights, appliances, and security systems with ease.

Electrical Panel Upgrades

We offer assistance with a variety of panel upgrades and repairs, including: Panel refurbishments, Distribution panel enhancements, Capacity expansions, Transitioning outdated fuse boxes to circuit breakers, Routine inspections, Modernizing breaker boxes, etc.

We provide expert consultations to help you plan and execute your electrical projects. This means professional advice for your home or business and the help that you need.
